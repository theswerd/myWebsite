About Us:

Benjamin Swerdlow is the head developer and project leader for SAMOHI Connect. He created it to help is school as a freshman, and the school adopted it as official when he was a sophomore.

Jessica Golden is the graphic designer for SAMOHI Connect. She created our logo, and was a huge help with development.

Features:

SAMOHI Connect is built by SAMOHI Students to help SAMOHI Students. It is your personal assistant during your time at SAMOHI.

ASB Events

SAMOHI Connect works with ASB to bring upcoming ASB events to you before anybody else.

School Calendar

The School Calendar takes data live from the official SAMOHI Calendar. It formats it and brings it straight to you.

Personal Checklist

Remembering homework is hard, SAMOHI Connect's got your back. Your personal checklist is there to help you with all your school work.

SAMOHI News

Don't know whats happening at SAMOHI. SAMOHI Connect brings the news to you.

School Map

Not sure where to go? SAMOHI Connect helps you see school in focus.

Schedule

Class is hard enough without having to memorize a bunch of schedules. SAMOHI Connect's got you, with today's schedule at your fingertips.

Staff Directory

Need to contact your teachers?Its never been easier than with SAMOHI Connect.

Clubs

Wanna join a club? See the Official SAMOHI Club list on SAMOHI Connect.
